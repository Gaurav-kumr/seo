What is Favicon?

A favicon, short for "favorite icon," is a small, typically square image or icon associated with a website. It is displayed in the browser's address bar, tabs, bookmarks, and other areas to visually represent a particular website. Favicons serve as a branding element and help users easily identify and distinguish websites when they have multiple tabs open or when bookmarking pages.
